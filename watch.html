Questions

The honest answers.

What does DexlyyWatch actually watch?

The public model surface of every major lab — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, Meta, DeepSeek, Mistral and Alibaba (Qwen), plus emerging players like Moonshot, Zhipu and MiniMax. We check APIs, model registries and changelogs continuously and confirm a release before alerting.

How fast will I hear about a drop?

Paid tiers get an instant email the same minute a model goes live. The free Watch tier is limited to the first 200 claimed seats and hears in a batched digest shortly after. SMS and voice calls are on the roadmap and will land for paid users first.

What's in the day-zero snapshot?

The launch numbers, pricing, context window and API id, pulled into one card the moment a model is detected — and slotted into a side-by-side against the current leaders so you can judge it quickly.

Do paid plans include a dashboard?

Yes. After PayPal approval, Pro and Squadron buyers land in a private dashboard where they confirm the alert email, choose lab and capability filters, and compare AI models side by side. Squadron also gets team routing fields for Slack and webhooks. Phone/SMS settings will appear when those channels go live.

Is it a subscription?

Pro and Squadron are simple monthly or annual plans — cancel anytime. The Watch tier is free for the first 200 seats only; once they are claimed, new users choose a paid plan.

Are you affiliated with these AI labs?

No. DexlyyWatch is an independent monitor and is not affiliated with, endorsed by or operated by any AI lab. We simply track what they ship so you don't miss it.

How accurate is the data?

Releases and details are drawn from official lab announcements and registries, and marked with their date. Where a figure is vendor-reported rather than independently verified, we say so. The radar reflects what we can confirm.
