FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is Dexlyy Shot different from Snipping Tool?

Snipping Tool captures pixels. Dexlyy Shot captures meaning — AI Ask, Smart Capture, multi-snip, scrolling capture, pin-to-screen, and a searchable history, all in one calm app.

Is Dexlyy Shot really a one-time purchase?

Yes. Pro is a $39 lifetime license for up to 3 computers — not a monthly subscription. You pay once inside the app and keep unlimited captures plus hosted AI features.

Why does Windows SmartScreen warn me?

We're a small studio and haven't code-signed our installer yet. The app is safe — click More info → Run anyway. Code signing is on the way.

What counts toward the free daily limit?

Each screenshot counts — including each snip in a multi-snip session. Combining them still uses one capture per region you selected.

Do I need an account?

Yes — a free account tracks your daily quota and unlocks Pro when you upgrade. Sign in with email or Google right inside the app.
