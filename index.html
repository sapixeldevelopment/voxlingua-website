FAQ

Questions, answered.

What makes VoxLingua different?

Three things: real-time dictation and translation across 100+ languages, a genuinely generous free plan with 3,000 words a week, and Pro from just $7/mo annually. More capability, an honest price.

Does it really work in every app?

Yes — VoxLingua runs system-wide. If you can put a cursor in it, you can dictate into it: email, Slack, Notion, Google Docs, code editors, your browser, and more, on macOS, Windows, and Linux.

Is my voice data private?

Always. Audio is encrypted in transit, never sold, and you can delete everything in one click. Free processes speech on your device; Pro adds cloud mode for higher-quality hosted transcription.

How accurate is the transcription?

Very high accuracy on clean audio, and it improves as it learns your vocabulary, names and accent. Custom dictionaries on Pro push it even higher.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel in two clicks and you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No for Free — start dictating in 100+ languages right away. Upgrade to Pro anytime for unlimited words, on-device mode, and translation.
